Manual Settings

Select this setting to use the color matching and adjustment methods provided with the printer driver.

Advanced Settings

Display the following options by clicking the arrow mark.

Mode

Use the Mode box to select one of the following modes (available modes differ depending on the product's model).
Item
Explanation
EPSON Vivid
Select this setting to enrich the blue and green tones in printouts.
Because this setting reproduces blue and green tones that cannot be displayed by your monitor, your printouts may differ from the screen image when this function is selected.
EPSON Standard
Increases the contrast in images. Use this setting for color photographs.
Adobe RGB
Matches image color to the Adobe RGB.

Gamma

Gamma is the unit that indicates the relation between input value and output value of the image gradation. Changing this value changes how the brightness of the midtone will look.
2.2
Select this to match image colors with other sRGB/Adobe RGB devices.
1.8
Select this to print images with a softer appearance than the setting described in 2.2.

Fix Red-Eye

Automatically detects red-eye on the scanned or digitally captured photo data, and then may improve or fix it when you print. This feature does not make changes on the original data, but only effects the prints.

Brightness

Changes the overall lightness or darkness within a range of -25 to +25%.

Contrast

Magnifies or minimizes the difference between the bright and dark parts within a range of -25 to +25%.

Saturation

Modifies the vividness of color within a range of -25 to +25%. The lower the saturation, the duller the colors. The higher the saturation, the more vivid the colors.

Cyan/Magenta/Yellow

Adjusts the amount of each of the three primary colors within a range of -25 to +25%.

PhotoEnhance/Fix Photo

This feature is best for printing images captured using a video camera, digital camera, or scanner. This feature produces sharper images and more vivid colors by automatically adjusting the contrast, saturation, and brightness of the original image data.
Note:
Printing with this feature selected may take longer depending on your computer system and the amount of data contained in your image.

Scene Correction

Select the appropriate scene type for your photos.
Item
Explanation
Auto Correct
Automatic setting to produce the photos.
People
Enhances the skin tone in photos of people.
Landscape
Enhances the colors of the sky and greenery in landscape photos.
Night Scene
Adjusts the contrast in night scenes.
Sepia
Applies a sepia tone to your photos.
Gray
Converts image to grayscale.

Fix Red-Eye

Automatically detects red-eye on the scanned or digitally captured photo data, and then may improve or fix it when you print. This feature does not make changes on the original data, but only effects the prints.

Digital Camera Correction

If you are printing photos taken with a digital camera, select this check box to give your printed photos the smooth, natural appearance of photographs taken with a film camera. Select Smooth Skin to adjust the image to make skin color smoother.

Off (No Color Adjustment)

The printer driver does not adjust the color. Choose this option only when making a ColorSync profile for this printer. When printing normally, choose one of the other modes.
Note:
This setting is not available when the application specifies that the printer driver handles color management.
